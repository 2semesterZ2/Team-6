Monthly Competition
Every month several winners are selected.
100 Points – Best Rated Tutorial.
150 Points – Most Viewed Tutorial
100 Points – Bauhaus Favorite Tutorial

Besides the Monthly competitions, you can still get points for Uploading Useful Tutorials, getting upvotes and joining competitions.
