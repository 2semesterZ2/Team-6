How to fix a squeeky door

A squeaky door can be really annoying, and wake up whoever you live with. In this tutorial you will need a screwdriver and some WD-40. First apply some WD-40 to the area shown below, and open/close your door for two minutes. Afterwards locate the screws (either on top or below). Tighten up the screws if they’re loose. don’t tighten the screws too much, make sure they are tight, but not unmovable.
